## The compounding loop (state it, don't just assert it)

The artifact is not the point of the loop; **the calibration is.** Each pass writes back: candidate
log → falsification log → three-axis readings → bet sheet → bet-retrospective → emergence log. The
**bet-retrospective** is the keystone: recording *why* a direction was cut (not just that it was)
turns abandonment from "a loss" into "freed bandwidth," and over many passes the hit rate and abandon
rate become a **self-calibrating judgment loop** — the second year's reading is structurally sharper
than the first's. That compounding context, not any single portfolio, is what a rival cannot copy.
